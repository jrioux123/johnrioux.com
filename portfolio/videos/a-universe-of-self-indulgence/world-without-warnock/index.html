World Without Warnock

January 2017

In my senior year of high school—the week of mid-terms, to be exact—my friend Jack Warnock went away on a week-long community service trip. Jack was the third member of a group chat I had with our friend Tom that we created about a year prior called “Big Trouble in Little Caesars” (for reasons not worth explaining), and in his absence, we felt it necessary to send him a ridiculously convoluted artistic tribute. Instead of studying for mid-terms, we spent the week exploring the capabilities of an instant messaging app as a storytelling device.
