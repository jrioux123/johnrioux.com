Arena of the Elders

2018 - present
About the game

Arena of the Elders is a competitive strategy board game created by Eddie Derhak and myself in 2018. It was a project that we'd been tinkering with for years beforehand, starting all the way back in middle school. But in 2018, we decided to bring it into publication. We founded Frogbox Entertainment and began working on a final product. Eddie was the lead designer of the game's mechanics, while I served as the game's illustrator and graphic designer.

The Starter Set

Arena of the Elders revolves around an assortment of fantasy characters. For the release of the Arena of the Elders Starter Set, we created nine for players to choose from. (We ended up cutting the ninth, Welklen, from the release.)
