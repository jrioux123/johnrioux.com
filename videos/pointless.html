Short film

Pointless

May 29, 2015
Synopsis

Michael Smith needs a pencil. Now, Michael could just go out and buy a box of pencils, but who uses money anymore, right? "Pointless" follows Michael's descent into a life of pencil-based organized crime.

Cast and crew

Written and Directed by John Rioux

Starring Alex Britton, Stefan Joyce, Rand Nohr, Timmy Okeny, and Phil Hobby

Casting by Alex Britton

Executive Producers Dan Wolotsky and Mark Melnicove
